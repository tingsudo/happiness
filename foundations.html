01 Foundations of Happiness

A Teen's Guide to Understanding What Really Makes Us Feel Good Inside

😊 What Is Happiness?

Happiness isn't just about being in a good mood or having fun. It's a mix of:

  • Positive emotions – Feeling good, enjoying life
  • Engagement – Being deeply involved in what you do
  • Relationships – Having supportive and loving people around
  • Meaning – Feeling like your life has a purpose
  • Accomplishments – Reaching goals and growing from the experience

This comes from the PERMA model by psychologist Martin Seligman.

💡 Real happiness = not just pleasure, but a life full of meaning and connection!

🧠 What Makes Us Happy?

According to science, happiness is shaped by three things:

  • 🧬 50% Genetics – Some people are naturally more upbeat
  • 🌍 10% Life circumstances – Where you live, how much money you have, etc.
  • 🌟 40% Our actions and mindset – What we do matters most!

Things you can do to feel happier:

  • Show kindness
  • Practice gratitude
  • Set meaningful goals
  • Spend time with friends or pets
  • Get involved in something bigger than yourself

🔄 Is Happiness Forever?

Nope, and that's totally normal.

  • Happiness goes up and down — that's part of being human!
  • Your brain releases "feel good" chemicals like dopamine and serotonin during happy moments.
  • Every culture experiences happiness differently — some value peace and harmony, others value individual joy.

🎢 What's the Difference Between Pleasure, Joy, and Fulfillment?

Type Feels Like... Lasts How Long? Comes From...
Pleasure Tasty snacks, warm blankets 🧁 Short-term Senses, comfort, external things
Joy Laughing with friends 💬 Medium Connection, values, meaningful acts
Fulfillment Deep satisfaction 🌱 Long-term Purpose, growth, helping others

🌈 A happy life includes all three — not just one.
